Mages

Scholars, collectors, armies of knights, all started year-long campaigns to record the new spells of the Gatling Grimoire. Now, groups are fighting eachother, using their newfound spells to steal patterns from them and complete their collection.

Only four, maybe less mages remain. They challenged each other in one last duel to decide who will conquer the Gatling Grimoire.
