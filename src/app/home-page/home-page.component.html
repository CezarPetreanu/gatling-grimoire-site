Gatling Grimoire

The bullet-dodging tabletop game

Gatling Grimoire is a fantasy duelling game in which the players’ dice rolling skills are put to the test against various attacks and spell patterns.

In a duel, it’s up to your strength and dexterity how you get out of the firefight: you can plunge straight into danger, play it safe, or dodge the bullets in style.
